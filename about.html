Frequently Asked Questions

Q. What the heck is a Cryptocurency?

A Cryptocurrency is a digital asset designed to work as a medium of exchange that uses strong cryptography to secure financial transactions, control the creation of additional units, and verify the transfer of assets.

Q. What is Cryptocurency Mining?

Cryptocurrency mining, or cryptomining, is a process in which transactions for various forms of cryptocurrency are verified and added to the blockchain digital ledger. It is also known as cryptocoin mining, altcoin mining, or Bitcoin mining.

Q. What is IncVest?

IncVest is an online platform that can be used by Miners to keep an account of their mined currencies and by newbies who want to learn how the cryptocurency market changes.

Q. How IncVest obtains the real-time prices of cryptocurencies?

We use the CoinMarketCap API for obtaining the prices of active cryptocurencies in real-time.

Q. How can it useful for a Miner or a Wannabe Miner?

IncVest provides a platform where miners can store the information of their mined cryptocurencies and have a general overview, of the invested money and Current Market price and profits/losses etc, any time. And wannabe miners, before starting actual mining, can get the hang of the cryptocurency market with our platform where they can invest virtual money, provided by us, in cryptocurencies.

Q. Is IncVest and its services free?

Yes. IncVest and its services are absolutely free for everyone, be it a single person or a group.
